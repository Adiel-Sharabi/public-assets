TacticalDrone Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

TacticalDrone is an FPV video viewer and recorder. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal information. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising, no crash reporting.

Device Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • USB Accessory - To receive video from connected FPV goggles.
  • Storage - To save recorded video files locally on your device.

Recorded Video

Video recordings are stored locally on your device. They are never uploaded or shared by the app. You control your recordings entirely.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data.

Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any data from anyone, including children of any age.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here.
